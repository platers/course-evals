Course Evaluations

Github

Course evaluation results for the Faculty of Arts & Science (Undergraduate Programs) are displayed here.
Responses range from 1 to 5, with 1 as the lowest rating and 5 as the highest rating.
To refine your search, simply type queries into the search bars.
In the group by drop down you can merge rows with the same code/deptartment/instructor.
Click any column header to sort the data using that column. Click again to reverse the sort order.
Tip: Filtering course codes by "CSC3" will only show 3rd year CS courses
